Type

Over the years 2 types of tutorials emerged. Playback-TASing is the act of recording keyboard and mouse inputs, and playing them back. This is the type that is usually associated with TASing in other games, played with an emulator.

Non-Playback-TASing emerged as an easier to learn alternative, that is less optimized than Playback TASing Plus there is still a lack of tools regarding Playback TASing, so long story short: Start with Non-Playback.
