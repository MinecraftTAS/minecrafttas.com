TAS-Battle

What is TAS-Battle?

TAS-Battle is a PvP mode where players fight each other in popular gamemodes
such as skywars and FFA, with a mod to slowdown the game (Often at 1/5 of the normal speed).

Fights can involve much smarter and riskier strategies that would not be possible with real-time reflexes. For some examples of TAS-Battle, click here!
